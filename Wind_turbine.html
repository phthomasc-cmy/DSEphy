üìö The Theory

1. Kinetic Energy of Air

Moving air has mass m and velocity v. Its energy is:

KE = ½ mv2

2. The Cylinder of Air

Imagine a cylinder of air hitting the blades.
Mass per second (m/t) = Density (ρ) × Area (A) × Speed (v).

m/t = ρAv

3. The Power Formula ‚ö°

If we combine the energy and mass formulas, we get the Power equation. Note that Power depends on velocity cubed (v3)!

P = ½ η ρ A v3

Where:
P = Power (Watts)
η (Eta) = Efficiency (0.0 to 0.59)
ρ (Rho) = Air Density (kg/m3)
A = Area swept by blades (m2)
v = Wind Speed (m/s)
